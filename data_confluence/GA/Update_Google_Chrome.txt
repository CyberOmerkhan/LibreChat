Update the Google Chrome Browser
Open Google Chrome on your Windows or Mac computer.
In the upper right corner, click on the three vertical dots icon.
In the resulting drop-down menu, select the
Help
>
About Google Chrome
menu options. Simply opening this page will initiate the update process.
Once the browser updates, you might need to click on the
Relaunch
button to reopen Chrome.
If the Update Fails
If the update fails, it is recommended that you download and install the latest version of Chrome. Start by opening this site
https://www.google.com/chrome/browser
Click on the
DOWNLOAD CHROME
button.
Open the installer and follow the instructions to complete the installation of Chrome.
