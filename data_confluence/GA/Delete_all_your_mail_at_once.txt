Title: Delete all your mail at once
URL: https://swatkb.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/GA/pages/19660844
Version: 7
---

Choose the
All Mail
folder from the menu on the lefthand side of your screen. You may need to select "More" to view this option.
Check the select box in the upper lefthand corner to select all emails.
You can also select "Read", "Unread", "Starred", "Unstarred" by clicking on the small arrow to the right of the checkbox
Click the "
Select all X messages in All Mail"
link (where
X
is the number of emails displayed).
Click the Trash icon to delete all emails.
