Title: What Google services are available with my Swarthmore Google account?
URL: https://swatkb.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/GA/pages/19661115
Version: 5
---

When logged into your Google account, you can see what current services are available by clicking on the nine-dot menu towards the upper right-hand portion of your screen.  Available services include Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Docs, Sheets and Slides, among others.  Contact
support@swarthmore.edu
if you have questions about the availability of a specific Google service.
At times, you may come across links to Google services that we aren't offering. In these cases, you should see a "Service not available" message.
