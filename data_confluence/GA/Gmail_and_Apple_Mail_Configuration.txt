BLOCK
Configuring email with Apple Mail in macOS.
Add an Account
Make sure your operating system is up to date.
Select the Apple Menu, then select
System Settings
.
In the lefthand sidebar, select
Internet Accounts
. You may need to scroll down to view this option.
Select
Add Account…
Select
Google.
Select
Open Browser
.
Type in your full Swarthmore email address and select
Next
.
On the Swarthmore login screen enter your Swarthmore username and password, then click
Login
.
Authenticate with Duo.
Select the apps you want to use with this account. Click
Done
.
Close the Internet Accounts window. Your mail is now set up.
