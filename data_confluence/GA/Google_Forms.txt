You can use Google Forms to create online surveys and quizzes, which can be sent to other people. Check
Google Support
often for updates.
Step 1: Set up a new form or quiz
Go to
forms.google.com
.
Select “Blank form”.
A new form will open.
Step 2: Edit and format your form
You can add, edit, or format text, images, or videos in a form. Click the links below to see how to perform each task.
Edit your form
Create a quiz with Google Forms
Choose where to save form responses
Step 3: Share your form with others
When you are ready, you can
send your form to others
and collect their responses.
