Title: Gmail Categories and Inbox Tabs

Version: 3

Gmail allows you to separate your Inbox into several categories that appear as tabs. These categories are Primary, Social, Updates, Forums, and Promotions. These allow Gmail to filter different emails into different tabs, which then allows you to focus your attention on each tab as needed. Gmail automatically sorts emails into categories for you, whether you choose to use the tabbed view or not.
How to Turn on the Category Inbox Tabs
Click on settings and then
See all settings
.
Click the
Inbox
tab
.
Make sure
Inbox type
is set to
Default
.
Check the box next to the category or categories you wish to have an inbox tab for.
Click
Save changes
at the bottom of the page.
Available Categories
Primary
- Person-to-person conversations and messages that don’t appear in other tabs.
Social
- Messages from social networks, media-sharing sites, online dating services, and other social websites.
Promotions
- Marketing, interests, social and political causes, and other promotional emails.
Updates
- Personal, auto-generated updates including confirmations, receipts, bills, and statements.
Forums
- Messages from online groups, discussion boards, and mailing lists.
Sometimes emails you expect to see in one tab appear in another. Below are some ways that you can update your settings to make sure emails are filtered into the correct categories.
Teach Gmail to Move Emails to a Different Tab
Right-click on the message and select
Move to tab
, then choose the category you want to move it to.
A message will appear in the bottom left-hand corner of the window asking if this should be done for all email from that address -- click
Yes
.
Create a Filter That Moves Emails to a Different Tab
From within the email, click the icon with three vertical dots and select
Filter messages like these
.
Make sure the
From
field has the correct address and click
Create filter
.
Check the
Categorize as
box and select the category you want to move it to.
You can also check
Apply filter to [#] matching conversations
box to have existing emails re-categorized.
Click
Create filter
.
Turn Off Unwanted Tabs
You can turn off one or all tabs. The content that was included in each tab you turn off gets put into your Primary tab. If you turn off all tabs, all emails will go into your Primary tab and be available when you view your Inbox.
Click on settings and then
See all settings
.
Click the
Inbox
tab.
Uncheck the category or categories you would like disabled.
Click
Save changes
at the bottom of the page.
