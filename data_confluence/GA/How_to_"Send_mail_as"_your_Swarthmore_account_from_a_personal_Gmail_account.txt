Title: How to "Send mail as" your Swarthmore account from a personal Gmail account
URL: https://swatkb.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/GA/pages/19660917
Version: 4
---

If you have set your Swarthmore email to forward to your personal Gmail account and wish to also send mail as your Swarthmore address from your personal Gmail, here is how you set up the “Send mail as” feature.
Please Note
These sections should be completed in the order they appear on this page in order to ensure you successfully set up Send Mail As.
From Your Swarthmore Account
Access the Relevant Settings
Browse to
https://myaccount.google.com/
Click
Security
on the left
Enable Less Secure Apps
Under the
Less secure app access
section, click
Turn on access (not recommended)
Make sure
Allow less secure apps
is set to
ON
May be sent to the Swarthmore login page, in order to continue
Click the left arrow at the top of the window to return to the security settings
Turn On 2-Step Verification
Under the
Signing in to Google
section, click
2-Step Verification
In the new window, click the
Get Started
button
Enter your phone number and click
Next
If your phone is already listed there, click
Continue
Google will send a message asking you to verify that you signed in from that device
Next, you will be prompted to add a backup device
You will get a text message on your phone with a verification code; enter this code (omit the
G-
part and just enter the six digits) and click
Next
Click
Turn On
Click the left arrow at the top of the window to return to the security settings
Set up an App Password
Under the
Signing in to Google
section, click
App Passwords
From the
Select app
drop-down, choose
Mail
From the Select device drop-down, choose
Other
(Custom name)
Provide a name of your choosing (for example:
Gmail
), and click
Generate
A 16 character password will appear: This is the password you will need to use (without the spaces) to set up 'Send mail as' (note that if you copy it with CTRL-C, it will not include the spaces).
Click
Done
From Your Personal Gmail Account
Enable IMAP
From your inbox, go to your settings by clicking the gear icon in the upper right of the window and then click
See all settings
Go to the
Forwarding and POP/IMAP
tab
Under the
IMAP access
section, make sure
Enable IMAP
is selected and click
Save Changes
Setup Send Mail As
Go to the
Accounts and Import
tab
Under the
Send mail as
setting, click
Add another email address
Enter your name and Swarthmore email address, and click
Next Step
Enter the 16 character password that was generated when you set up an app password
Click
Add Account
, and in the next window click
Close window
A confirmation email will be sent to your Swarthmore account; click the link that's in it, then click the
Confirm
button in the page it opens
