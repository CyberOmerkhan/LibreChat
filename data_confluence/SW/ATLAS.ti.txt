How to get a copy?
While any faculty, staff, or student at Swarthmore can use ATLAS.ti on their personal and/or College computer, as well as the web version, please note that licenses are carefully tracked. Also, note that this software is purchased by ITS primarily for teaching purposes. While we support its use generally, it may be necessary to limit users if it becomes necessary during classes that require all the licenses to be available. We strongly encourage faculty who need this tool for their research to purchase their own licenses. Please visit our
Service Portal
to request a license code and download link.
