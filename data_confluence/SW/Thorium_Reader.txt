About
According to Thorium Reader, it “is the EPUB reader of choice for Windows 10 and 11, MacOS and Linux.
This EDRLab application is in constant development and aims at becoming a reference for accessing EPUB 3 publications in reflow or fixed-layout format, audiobooks and visual narratives, PDF documents and DAISY 2.02 and 3 accessible ebooks, LCP protected or not.”
Download
Download and install Thorium for Linux or Mac.
Windows users can find
Thorium in the Microsoft Apps Store
or download the
Thorium installer for Windows from Github
.
Installation Instructions
Open the installer you downloaded from the Thorium site or Microsoft Apps Store.
Once the download is complete, click on the installation file.
Follow installation instructions if necessary.
