Description
Google NotebookLM is a tool that aims to enhance the note-taking and research process by leveraging Artificial Intelligence (AI). It allows users to upload files, videos, webpages, and audio files and create summaries, study guides, and other methods of understanding the content.
Getting Started
Go to
notebooklm.google.com
and log in with your Swarthmore Google account
Create a new notebook
Add content: upload files or enter URLs for websites or YouTube videos
NotebookLM will take a few minutes to summarize the content. Select the available options to create summaries, ask questions, or generate a podcast.
Further Support
See Google's
NotebookLM support pages
Notes
When used with a Swarthmore Google account, NotebookLM will not train on your content
