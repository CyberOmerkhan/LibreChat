Description
Padlet is a easy-to-use service to create bulletin boards to collect, organize, and present information.  Often used in student projects to share
images, maps, audio, and video.  Faculty can create a shared board for students to post information.  Its strength is simplicity.
Example Padlet board
The Padlet Sandbox feature is a “collaborative digital canvas with tools for drawing, writing, and adding media.”
Login
Anyone with a Swarthmore account can log into the paid version of Padlet at
swarthmorecollege.padlet.org/auth/login
.  Use Google to complete the login process.
Faculty can set up
a link from Moodle to automatically enroll students in a Padlet
.
