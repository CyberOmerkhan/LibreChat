Sign In With the Zoom Application
1)  Open your Zoom application.
(If you need to download and install the application, go to
https://swarthmore.zoom.us
. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click "
Download Zoom Client
".)
2)  Select "
Sign In with SSO
".
3)  Enter "
Swarthmore
" as the company domain. This will take you to the Swarthmore login page. Enter your Swarthmore username and password > then  go through the Duo process.
4)  You can also sign in via a web browser to schedule or start a meeting. Go to
https://swarthmore.zoom.us
and sign in with Swarthmore College username and password and go through the Duo process.
