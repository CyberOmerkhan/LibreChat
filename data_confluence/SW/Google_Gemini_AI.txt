Description
Gemini is Google’s Artificial Intelligence (AI) tool. The service is available as a web chatbot or mobile app.
When logged in with your Swarthmore Google account, Google will not use your information to train their models. If you use Gemini with a personal account, Google may use your data for training purposes.
