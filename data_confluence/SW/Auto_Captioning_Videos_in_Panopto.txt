Download the Captions File
After you've applied the captions and would like to download the captions file, hover your cursor over the video session and select
Settings.
Choose
Captions
from the menu on the left and then select
Download File.
Open the downloaded file using Notepad, Word, or another text editor and optionally delete the text that includes timestamps.
