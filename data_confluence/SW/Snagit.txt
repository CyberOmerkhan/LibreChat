When you need to quickly capture your screen, including capturing all of a long or wide web page, or create a short video, or even convert a short video into an animated GIF without a lot of fuss, then
Snagit
is the tool for you.
Installation Instructions
Windows:
Download the package to your desktop and double click to install.
Mac:
Download the appropriate package to your desktop and unzip.
After either installation, you will need to provide a license code and follow the following instructions:
How to enter your Snagit License Key:
https://support.techsmith.com/hc/en-us/articles/360021040791-Snagit-Entering-Your-Software-Key
Download
Contact
support@swarthmore.edu
for a link to the software download and a license code.
ITS tracks installations of this software due to licensing restrictions.
