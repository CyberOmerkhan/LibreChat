Windows Installation Instructions
Click on the Windows menu.  Type
\\swat-print
in the search box
and press Enter. If prompted for credentials, enter GARNET\username in the username field, and your password.
Find the printer you're looking for.
For ease of use, the printer naming convention is "department-building and room number", example "its-beardsley110"
If you do not know, or cannot determine, the name of the printer, please contact the Help Desk for assistance.
Double-click on the printer (it will install automatically)
