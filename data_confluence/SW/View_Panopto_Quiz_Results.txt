It is possible to insert quizzes within a Panopto video. This page explains how to see the results of the quiz.
Login to Panopto at
panopto.swarthmore.edu
.
Hover
over the video with the embedded quiz (should display menu options).
Click on
Settings.
Click on the
Quiz Results
tab.
Review
results summary or
download
a copy. The downloaded file is in CSV format.
Read more about
Panopto Quiz Results
from Panopto's support documentation.
