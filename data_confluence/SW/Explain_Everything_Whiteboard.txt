Description
Explain Everything Whiteboard is
a subscription-based digital whiteboard app
that can be used to record a presentation or collaborate on a shared project. Explain Everything Whiteboard has collaboration features as well as a cloud storage feature called
Explain Drive. You can access your Explain Everything Drive account via
drive.explaineverything.com
. L
og in via Google using your Swarthmore email address.
