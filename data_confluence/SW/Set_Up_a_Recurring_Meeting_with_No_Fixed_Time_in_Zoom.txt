Set up a recurring meeting with No Fixed Time
Sign into swarthmore.zoom.us using your Swarthmore credentials.
Click on the
Meetings
tab from the left-hand menu.
Click on
Schedule a meeting
from the top right.
Enter the information for the meeting.
The
Topic
will be the name of the meeting. If it is for a class, be sure to title it with the name of the course and the semester the meeting is for.
Select the
Recurring meeting
check box.
Select
No Fixed Time
from the
Recurrence
dropdown menu.
Set meeting options that are applicable.
Click
Save
when finished.
Share the zoom meeting invitation through your Moodle course, calendar invite, or email.
