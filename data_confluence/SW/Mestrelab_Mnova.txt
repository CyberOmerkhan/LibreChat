License File
After installation, apply the license files below on the first launch.   Computers must be connected to the campus network to use the software.
License Files
To apply new license files on an existing install, download the license files and place them in the following directory:
Win:  C:\Program Files\MestReNova\Mestrelab Research S.L.\licenses OR C:\Program Files (x86)\MestReNova\Mestrelab Research S.L.\licenses
Mac: \Library\Application Support\Mestrelab Research S.L.\Mestrenova\licenses
