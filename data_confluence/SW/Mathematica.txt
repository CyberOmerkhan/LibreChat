Installation Instructions
To install Mathematica, log into the
Wolfram Products Page
with your Swarthmore email address. You will be prompted to log in with your Swarthmore credentials. From there you can download the installer package.
You will also use your Swarthmore email address to log into Mathematica once it is installed.
Mathematica Online
To access Mathematica online using a browser:
Go to
http://mathematica.wolframcloud.com
to start using Mathematica online.   Log in with your Swarthmore credentials.
