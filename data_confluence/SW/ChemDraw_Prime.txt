Installation Instructions
Swarthmore College has a site license for ChemDraw Prime. Anyone with a Swarthmore email address is covered by the site license and is able to install ChemDraw Prime on any school or personally-owned computer. To download the latest version follow the instructions below:
Go to the
ChemDraw subscription site
Search for Swarthmore College
Create an account using your Swarthmore email (or log in if you have an existing account)
Select your preferred software - there are downloadable and online versions
Follow the instructions to register and create an account.  Use your Swarthmore email.
Validate your account after you receive an account setup email.
Select the products you wish to download or access.
Swarthmore has a site license for ChemDraw Prime.  You may see other products listed (e.g. ChemDraw Professional), but they are available only to users that paid extra for access.
