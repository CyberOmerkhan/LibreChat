About
BeeLine Reader’s is a browser-based software suite that helps you read more quickly and easily by applying a line-wrapping color gradient to text. This technique improves visual tracking and focus to enable faster and easier reading. To learn more about BeeLine Reader, visit their
BeeLine Reader website
or see features
in The Atlantic
and
on NPR
.
BeeLine will work with macOS, Windows, and Linux web browsers.
Swarthmore has a license agreement that gives all students, faculty, and staff access.
