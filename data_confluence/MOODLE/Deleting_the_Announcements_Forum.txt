Title: Deleting the Announcements Forum
URL: https://swatkb.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/MOODLE/pages/20906786
Version: 7
---

The Announcements Forum in Moodle is used to post class news and announcements and is added to all new courses automatically. If you prefer not use the Announcements Forum and want to get rid of it, you'll find that deleting from the front page appears to work, but the Forum will reappear when you refresh the page.
In previous versions of Moodle the Announcements forum was named "News Forum." The function is the same but the name was updated to provide a clearer indication of its purpose.
Instructions
To get rid of the Announcements Forum:
Turn on Edit Mode
Click the
three dots
button to the right of the
Announcements Forum
and choose
Delete
.
Click on the course
Settings
link below the course title
Find the
Appearance
section. Expand the Appearance menu items.
Set
Number of announcements
to 0.
Scroll to the bottom and click the blue
Save Changes
button.
If you have a
Latest news
block on the right hand side of the page, you should remove it since there will be no news to display.  See the
Moodle
help page
for instructions.
Multiple Copies of the Announcement or News Forums
If you import material from a previous semester's course, you may end up with multiple copies of Announcement and/or News forums.  Follow Step 1 and 2 above to remove all but one of the Announcement/News forum activities.
Getting the Announcements Forum Back
If you have Announcement-Forum-removers remorse, you can get the forum back by setting the
Number of announcements
in the course settings
to a non-zero value.
