Title: Embed a Panopto Video on a Moodle Page
URL: https://swatkb.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/MOODLE/pages/20908641
Version: 2
---

By default, Panopto recordings appear within the Panopto block on your Moodle course page. You can also embed the video anywhere in Moodle that has a rich-text editor. For example, you can go to any section or add a new page activity in Moodle and embed the video there.
To embed a video, go to a place where you can edit text and click on the green Panopto icon on the editor toolbar.  Find your video and select it to insert into the page.
