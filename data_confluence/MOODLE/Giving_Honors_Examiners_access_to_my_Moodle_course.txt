Title: Giving Honors Examiners access to my Moodle course
URL: https://swatkb.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/MOODLE/pages/20908401
Version: 13
---

Although it is technically possible to give a guest access to a Moodle course (see
), the
Honors Program
does not permit Honors Examiners to see the contents of a Moodle course in order to ensure that the examiner is not prejudiced by any student work that may be available in Moodle.  Contact the
Director of the Honors Program
for more details.
To share important readings and files from your course, you can download the files from Moodle then upload them to a Google Drive folder and share the folder with the Honors Examiner. For more information on downloading files from Moodle in bulk, see
.
