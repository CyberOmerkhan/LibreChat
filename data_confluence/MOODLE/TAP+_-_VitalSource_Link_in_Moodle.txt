As part of the Textbook Affordability Program (TAP), the Bookstore is making many textbooks available online through Moodle using the VitalSource service.  This e-books program is called TAP+. You may see a link in your course to TAP+ Books. This is how you and your students can access the e-books.
Adding a link to VitalSource
If your course uses a VitalSource e-book, adding a link in Moodle will give your students access to the content. To add a link:
Turn editing on
Add an activity or resource
Select
VitalSource
Give the activity a name such as “TAP+ Books”
Click
Save and return to course
