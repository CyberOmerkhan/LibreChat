Title: Making the gradebook visible to students

Version: 12
---

By default, students cannot see the gradebook.  To let students see their own grades follow the steps below. Students cannot see grades for other students.
Go to your course
Click
Settings
(under course title)
In the
Appearance
section, change
Show gradebook to students
to "
Yes
."
Scroll down and click
Save and display
