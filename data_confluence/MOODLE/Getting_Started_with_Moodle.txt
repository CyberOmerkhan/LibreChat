All courses and enrollments have been entered into Moodle, so instructors can log in and get started on their courses right away.
Go to
https://moodle.swarthmore.edu
.
Click on the
Log in
button and log in with your Swarthmore username and password.
If you have courses available, they will be shown in the middle of the screen. Select a course to enter it.
In a course, toggle the
Edit Mode
button (top right) to begin adding content.
See more Moodle resources by visiting our
Moodle Help
page.
