Moodle keeps tracks of user activity in Moodle.  To view information from the Moodle logs, click on
Reports
at the top of the course page.   There are a variety of reports on Moodle activity that are available.
Dates
- lists all of the course activities that have a date associated with them (ie. course start date, due dates, etc.)
Logs
- reports on all activity within the course
Live Logs
- quick report on course activity in the last hour
Activity report
- number of views for each activity in the course.  Does not include any user information.
Course participation
- report on if students view or post to a specific activity within the course over the past week.
Activity completion
- check boxes for each course activity for each participant
Roster
- course participants with photos (if available)
Statistics
- Nothing currently
