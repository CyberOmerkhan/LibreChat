In order to export all of the student grades from the grade book into an excel spreadsheet follow the steps below:
Click on
Grades
from the top of the page to go to the
Grader Report
Click on
Export
from the pulldown menu
Then select
Excel Spreadsheet
from the pulldown
Select Grade Items to be included
Click
Download
