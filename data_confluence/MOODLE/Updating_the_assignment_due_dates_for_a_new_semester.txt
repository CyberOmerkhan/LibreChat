Title: Updating the assignment due dates for a new semester
URL: https://swatkb.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/MOODLE/pages/20907120
Version: 6
---

After importing a course from a previous semester, all the due dates for assignments and other activities will be incorrect. It is possible to update all the dates for the class from the
Dates Report
.
To access this report:
Go to the course main page and click
Reports
(under course title)
→ Dates
.
Adjust the dates for each activity as needed.
Click
Save changes
when finished.
This report runs very slowly for large courses and may time out if there are many activities.
