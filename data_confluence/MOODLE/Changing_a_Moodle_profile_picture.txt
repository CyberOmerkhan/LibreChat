Title: Changing a Moodle profile picture
URL: https://swatkb.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/MOODLE/pages/20906758
Version: 7
---

To change your picture, log into Moodle then click your name in the top right of the screen
→
Profile → Edit Profile
(in the
User Details
section) and scroll down to the
User Picture
area.  Drag your desired image file into the
New Picture
box.  The file should be a .jpg or .png file format and will be cropped and resized to a 100x100 pixel square automatically.
Pictures are automatically loaded from college records each Fall. If you have loaded a different picture into Moodle it won't be overwritten, but if you have deleted your picture from Moodle, your ID picture will be reloaded into your profile.
