Title: Grade Essay Questions Within a Moodle Quiz
URL: https://swatkb.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/MOODLE/pages/20908307
Version: 4
---

Click the on the name of the quiz on the main course page
Click the
Results
tab at the top
Select
Manual grading
(from the dropdown menu)
On the next page, click the
Grade all
link in the row of the question that you would like to grade
Under the
Options
section, next to
Order attempts by
select
By student last name
or
By student first name
Click the
Change Options
button
Read responses and enter a grade (mark) for each one:
Student responses will appear in the top area in the essay answer section
You can leave optional feedback for your students in the comments section. You will need to have set up your
Quiz Settings
to allow your students to view this feedback after the Quiz is closed.
Enter your grades in the points box. Note that Moodle will provide the total number of possible points as a reference for you.
When you have entered all the grades and optional feedback for the selected question on the page, scroll down and click the
Save and go to next page
button. This will take you to the next student.
When you are done grading this question, scroll to the top of the page and click the
Back to the list of questions
link.
