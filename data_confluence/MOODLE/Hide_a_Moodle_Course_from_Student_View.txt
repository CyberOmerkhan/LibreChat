Title: Hide a Moodle Course from Student View

There may be cases where you'd like to hide your Moodle course from student view. For example, if you have combined multiple sections of a course, you may want to hide the sections you are not using so students won’t get confused. There are also times at the end of the semester where you’d want to hide the course in order to protect any copyrighted material.
Hide a Moodle course from student view
Go to your course in Moodle
Click the
Settings
link under the course title
Under the
General
heading, locate the dropdown menu to the right of
Course visibility
Choose
Hide
Scroll to the bottom of the page and choose
Save and display
You may be interested in other Moodle permissions or restrictions
Making a course available to students or guests
Hiding selected Moodle items from guests
Restricting access to items to certain members of the course
