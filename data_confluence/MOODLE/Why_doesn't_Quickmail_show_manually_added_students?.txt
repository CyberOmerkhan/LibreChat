Title: Why doesn't Quickmail show manually added students?
URL: https://swatkb.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/MOODLE/pages/20906391
Version: 6
---

If you have manually added students to your course but they don't show up in the Quickmail list of potential recipients, the students have probably been added to the course without a role.  To fix the problem, go to the
Participants
page.  Find the user that is not showing up and verify that the
Roles
column is empty for that student.  Click on the Pencil icon in the Roles column and add the appropriate role.  You should now be able to email the user using Quickmail.
