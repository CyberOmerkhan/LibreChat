Title: Hiding the Moodle grade course total
URL: https://swatkb.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/MOODLE/pages/20906766
Version: 7
---

Some faculty use the gradebook in Moodle but do not use it to calculate the final grade or prefer not to show students their total grade before the end of the semester.  In these cases, it is useful to allow students to see grades for individual items or categories but hide the course total grade.  To hide the course total, follow the following procedure:
From your main course page, click
Grades
(under course title)
Select
Gradebook Setup
from the pulldown menu
Scroll to the bottom of the grade setup table and click on the
...
button
Select
Hide
