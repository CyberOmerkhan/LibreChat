To make a course stand out on the Moodle home page, it is possible to upload an image for the course thumbnail.  When viewing the Moodle Dashboard in the card or summary view, the image will be shown.  If you don't set an image, Moodle picks a default abstract image.  Learn more about the course overview listing on our
Moodle Dashboard help page
.
Examples
The first course has a custom course image.  The other courses have a default Moodle image.

Instructions
Go to your course and click the
Settings
link below the course title.
Go to the
Description
section and upload your image to the
Course image
section.
Save the updated setting and go to your
course listing
to see the results
