If you are requested by ITS to provide a copy of an email with "headers displayed," please refer to the section below that corresponds to how you read your email.
To extract email headers from Gmail:
Open the email message.
Click the three vertical dots adjacent to the
Reply
link in the upper-right corner of the email message.
Click
Show Original
.
Copy and paste the information from the new window into an email.
To extract email headers from Thunderbird:
Double-click the message to open it in a separate window.
From the View menu, select
Headers
>
All
.
Click the
Forward
button to forward the email.
(You can then go back to the View menu and select
Headers > Normal
.
To extract email headers from Apple Mail:
Select the email in the Inbox.
Open the
View
menu, select
Message > Raw Source
.
Copy the resulting text.
Paste the text into an email.
