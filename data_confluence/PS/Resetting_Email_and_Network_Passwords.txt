Set Your Password for the First Time
Set First PW
INLINE
Visit
password.swarthmore.edu
and click the
Claim Your Identity
link at the bottom of the login screen.
You will see a Welcome screen, click
Next
.
Enter the username and employee or student ID number provided to you by the college. Click
Submit
.
You will be asked to review and agree to the Swarthmore College Acceptable Use Policy. A link will be provided for review. Once done, check the box to agree to the terms and click
Next
.
Enter at least one recovery option, but both are recommended. After you enter the option(s), click
Update
. The options include:
A non-Swarthmore email address
A non-Swarthmore mobile phone number
Set up your Duo 2-factor authentication device registration. You will be walked through the Duo set up process depending on the device type you chose.
You will need to install the Duo Mobile app to complete device registration. Contact the
Help Desk
if you are not able to install the Duo Mobile app.
You will be asked to enter a password. When you click into the text field, the requirements for setting a password at Swarthmore will show as a dropdown window below the text field. As you fulfill the requirements, they will automatically be checked on the list. Once you have a password that passes the checklist, re-enter it in the field below to confirm it. Click
Next
.
At any time you can click on the eye icon on the right-hand side of the text fields to see the password.
View our page on
tips for creating strong passwords
.
You will see a confirmation screen that you have completed the process to claim your account. Click
Complete
.
Reset Your Password
Reset Expired PW
INLINE
Visit
password.swarthmore.edu
and click the
Forgot Password
link.
You will see a welcome screen, click
Next
.
Enter your username and click
Submit
.
Choose the recovery option you want to use to verify your identity. You will be sent a verification code to the option you choose.
Enter the verification code and click
Verify
.
Authenticate with Duo.
You will be asked to enter a password. When you click into the text field, the requirements for setting a password at Swarthmore will show as a dropdown window below the text field. As you fulfill the requirements, they will automatically be checked on the list. Once you have a password that passes the checklist, re-enter it in the field below to confirm it. Click Next.
At any time you can click on the eye icon on the right-hand side of the text fields to see the password.
View our page on
tips for creating strong passwords
.
You will see a confirmation screen that your password has been reset. Click
Complete
.
Now that your password is changed, check out our page on
what you need to update after you change your password
.
Manage Your Recovery Information
Visit
password.swarthmore.edu
and login with your Swarthmore credentials.
Click on your initials in the top-right corner and choose
My Profile
.
Edit or enter your non-Swarthmore recovery options as necessary. Click
Submit
.
You will be returned to the Home screen.
Contact the Help Desk
If you run into any issues and are unable to set or reset your password, please contact the Help Desk.
