If you would like to take advantage of a Keeper password manager account to keep all of your work passwords secure and help create unique, strong passwords for all of your accounts - follow the simple process below!
On a laptop or desktop computer that is assigned to you/you own - not another person’s computer or a public computer (a computer lab or library computer):
Open your preferred web browser and go to
keepersecurity.com/vault
Choose “
Create an account
”
Enter your full Swarthmore email address, i.e. test1@swarthmore.edu
Choose “
Next
”
You will be redirected to the usual Swarthmore login page
Enter your username and password and choose “
Login
”
Authenticate with DUO
You will be redirected to your new Keeper vault
A pop-up will help walk you through setting up your account, including importing passwords. You can also check out our pages on
exporting your passwords from LastPass
and
importing passwords into Keeper with a CSV file
.
