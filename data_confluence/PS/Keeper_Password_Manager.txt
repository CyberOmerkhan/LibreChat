We are excited to announce that we have partnered with Keeper to provide a secure password manager for our employees. This service is voluntary, but it can help keep the various passwords you need secure, unique, and strong. Password managers are a safe and secure way to store your passwords, allowing you to make them unique and complex without having to remember them all.
At this time we do not provide paid Keeper accounts for Students. However, students are encouraged to sign up for a free Keeper account using a personal email to benefit from the added password security at
keepersecurity.com
.
