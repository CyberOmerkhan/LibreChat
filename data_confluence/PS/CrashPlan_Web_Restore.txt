To restore backed-up files, you can restore them from the web using the administration console.
Note:
Web restore is optimized for single files or small folders only.  The web restore will not work well for files over 1GB.
In a web browser, go to
console.us2.crashplan.com
Enter your email address click
Sign In,
and then log in
Authenticate with your credentials after being redirected to the Swarthmore login page
Go to
Devices
and select
Active
to list your active devices
Click the relevant device, which will display it:
Click the restore icon.
Select the files or folders to restore by checking the boxes next to the relevant items.
For Windows, your files will be under "C:\ → Users → <your username>".
For macOS, your files will be under "Users → <your username>".
After selecting the needed files, click
Restore
.
(Optional) Select a calendar date to restore a previous version of files (if the file has previous versions, it will have a small arrow to the left of the file).  Refer to the image below:
(Optional) Select
Display deleted files
to show files that have been deleted from this computer,
Display hidden files
to show files that are normally not visible, and/or
Full Paths
to specify the exact location of the file(s):
In the lower-left, click the
Click here to download
link to download the zip file containing your selected files
Most computers will unzip by double-clicking the downloaded file.
Please contact the Help Desk or put in a ticket at support.swarthmore.edu if you need assistance with the process.
