We have some exciting news to share about a new campus initiative. In order to better support remote and hybrid work, and in collaboration with our campus partners, ITS is working closely with Zoom to begin migrating our current Cisco Phone System to Zoom Phone.
The project will be implemented in multiple phases, beginning this fall. This summer, alongside ITS, we will also invite a few departments to join as early adopters. We anticipate that many members of our community will opt to utilize Zoom's virtual phone features via their computer or mobile device app rather than relying on physical desk phones.
For answers to frequently asked questions visit our
Zoom Phone Project FAQs
page. For questions regarding the project timeline, migration process, or training please visit the
Zoom Phone Project Rollout
page. You can also still view the
recorded Zoom Phone training sessions (GDoc)
- login is required.
We understand that transitioning to Zoom Phone represents a significant change from our current telecommunications system, and we are here to provide support. If you have any questions or concerns during the transition, please don't hesitate to contact us through our
ITS Service Portal
.
