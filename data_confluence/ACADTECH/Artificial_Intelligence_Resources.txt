Swarthmore provides Artificial intelligence (AI) tools to support teaching, learning, and research.
Tools
All listed tools are private and do not train on user data.
Text-Based
LibreChat
multi-model AI chatbot including access to ChatGPT and Claude.
Google Gemini AI
Google’s AI chatbot and mobile app
NotebookLM
Google’s service that uses your specific documents as a knowledge base to answer questions, summarize content, and generate ideas.
Image and Video
Firefly
generate images from a text description
Other
Zoom AI Companion
automatically generate meeting summaries and allow participants to questions about the meeting.
